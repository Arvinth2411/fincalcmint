    Privacy Policy

    FinCalc Mint ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website fincalcmint.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website. By using our site, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

    1. Information We Do NOT Collect

    FinCalc Mint is a calculation-only tool. All financial calculations (EMI, SIP, tax, GST, salary, etc.) are performed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. We do not receive, store, or have any access to the numbers you enter into our calculators. No calculation data is ever sent to our servers.

    We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information to use our calculators.

    2. Information Collected Automatically

    Like most websites, we may collect limited non-personally identifiable information automatically when you visit, including:

    • Log data: IP address (anonymised), browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and date/time of visit — collected by our web hosting server for security and performance purposes.
    • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device by your browser. See Section 4 for details.
    • Local Storage: We store your theme preference (light/dark mode) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

    3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

    We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet.

    Key points about Google AdSense on our site:

    • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
    • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
    • You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.
    • For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy for Partner Sites.

    5. How We Use Information

    The limited data we collect (server logs, ad analytics) is used solely to:

    • Monitor and improve the performance and security of our website

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every website you visit.

    7. Children's Privacy

    8. Data Security

    Since we do not collect or store your financial data, there is no risk of your calculation data being breached. For server log data that is automatically collected, we implement reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access.
